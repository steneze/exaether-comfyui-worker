1. Cadrage centré;the composition places the subject in the center of the frame with symmetrical balanced formal framing
2. Règle des tiers gauche;the composition follows the rule of thirds with the subject positioned on the left vertical third leaving breathing space on the right
3. Règle des tiers droit;the composition follows the rule of thirds with the subject positioned on the right vertical third leaving space on the left
4. Espace négatif haut;the composition includes significant negative space above the subject creating room to breathe with contemplative sky or ceiling emphasis
5. Espace négatif bas;the composition includes significant negative space below the subject creating an unusual grounded floor emphasis
6. Espace négatif gauche;the composition has significant empty space on the left with the subject on the right looking into the space creating anticipation
7. Espace négatif droit;the composition has significant empty space on the right with the subject on the left implying narrative direction and movement
8. Cadrage serré;the composition uses tight framing with minimal space around the subject creating intimate claustrophobic intensity
9. Cadrage aéré;the composition uses loose framing with generous space around the subject allowing breathing room with environmental context
10. Cadrage asymétrique;the composition uses deliberate asymmetrical framing creating tension with artistic off-balance composition
11. Cadrage diagonal;the composition places the subject or dominant lines on a diagonal creating dynamic energy and implied movement
12. Cadrage encadré;the composition uses a frame within frame with the subject seen through a doorway window or arch creating layered depth
13. Bord cadre;the composition places the subject at the edge of the frame with unusual cropping creating tension and incomplete view
14. Cadrage fragmenté;the composition shows only parts of the subject visible creating abstract mysterious partial framing
15. Cadrage symétrique;the composition uses perfect symmetrical framing with the subject centered and elements balanced creating formal powerful presence
