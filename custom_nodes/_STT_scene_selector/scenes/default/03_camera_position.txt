1. Face frontale;the camera is positioned directly in front with the subject facing the camera straight on in symmetrical confrontational or open composition
2. Trois-quarts gauche;the camera captures a three-quarter view from the left with the subject turned 30 to 45 degrees to the right in classic portrait angle
3. Trois-quarts droit;the camera captures a three-quarter view from the right with the subject turned 30 to 45 degrees to the left creating dynamic asymmetry
4. Profil gauche;the camera captures her left profile with the subject facing left and the camera at 90 degrees showing silhouette potential with dramatic effect
5. Profil droit;the camera captures her right profile in side view emphasizing the sculptural line of her nose and lips
6. Trois-quarts dos gauche;the camera is positioned at three-quarter back from the left with the subject turned away potentially looking over her right shoulder
7. Trois-quarts dos droit;the camera is positioned at three-quarter back from the right with the subject turned away potentially looking over her left shoulder
8. Vue de dos;the camera is positioned behind capturing the back of the subject with her facing away creating mystery with focus on hair and posture
9. Par-dessus épaule gauche;the camera is positioned over her left shoulder from behind creating an intimate following perspective
10. Par-dessus épaule droite;the camera is positioned over her right shoulder from behind creating a narrative viewpoint
11. Vue latérale gauche;the camera captures a full side view from the left perpendicular to the subject showing full body profile like a fashion walk angle
12. Vue latérale droite;the camera captures a full side view from the right showing her complete silhouette perpendicular to camera
13. Diagonale avant gauche;the camera is positioned at a diagonal front-left creating a dynamic angle between frontal and profile
14. Diagonale avant droit;the camera is positioned at a diagonal front-right creating an asymmetric engaging angle
15. Position orbite;the camera position suggests movement as if orbiting around the subject capturing her from a dynamic circling perspective
